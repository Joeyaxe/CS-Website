How long does it take for a M and M shell to dissolve in hot water?

Data Collection

First we started by opening a bag of M and Ms. Then we filled 3 cups full with really hot water and set them on the table. Then we took 1 M and M for each cup of water and put them in the cups of the water. Then we started a timer as soon as we put the M and Ms in the cups of water. After that, we waited until we saw that the M and M shells were dissolved in the hot water. Finally we recorded the times that it took for the M and M shells to dissolve and put those times on a Google spreadhseet.
